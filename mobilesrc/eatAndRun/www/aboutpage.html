Eat N' Run: Lunch Lady Madness

Gameplay

You will begin by being chased by a Lunch Lady. She is mad that you have skipped your lunch today. By making balanced healthy eating choices you will be able to outrun the Lunch Lady.

Objective

Eat what you want, keeping in mind that the choices that you make will impact your running ability. To be able to maximize your running potential, you will need to eat a balance of foods that are fun to eat and foods that are fun and healthy!

Rules

  • You need to be sure that the foods that you decide to eat are beneficial to your overall health.

  • If you eat foods that are fun, but do not eat anything except these red type foods, then your character will become slow and fat!

  • If you eat too many green type foods, then your character will become slow and tired!

  • If you eat a balance (indicated by the nutrition bar) of green and red type foods then you will reach the coveted maximum balance.

Scoring

In order to score higher in Eat n' Run: Lunch Lady Madness you need to maintain a healthy balance in addition to running as far as you can.

The more you play the better you will become at making healthy eating choices and you will score higher allowing you to rule the leaderboards!
