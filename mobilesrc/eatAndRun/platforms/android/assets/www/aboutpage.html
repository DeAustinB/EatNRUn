Gameplay

Eat food that gives you energy to run and keep enough distance between you and the lunch lady to avoid being caught.

Objective

Pick the proper food to eat that will keep the nutrition bar balanced to avoid being caught by the lunch lady before time runs out.

Rules

  • Don't get caught by the Lunch Lady.
  • Don't eat too much Red food.
  • Don't eat too much Green food.
  • Keep your nutritional bar balanced.

Scoring

When you eat, you score!
