Gameplay

Eat foods that give you energy to run and keep enough distance between you and the lunch lady to avoid being caught.

Objective

Decide the foods to eat that will keep the nutrition bar balanced between -----as your running pace slows or quickens to stay away from or be caught by the lunch lady before time runs out.

Rules

  • Eat foods that help you stay ahead of the lunch lady.
  • Red foods slow your run .
  • Green foods quicken your run.
  • When balance is achieved you will be rewarded with extra points, unlockables, and super avatar power .

Scoring

When you eat, you score!
