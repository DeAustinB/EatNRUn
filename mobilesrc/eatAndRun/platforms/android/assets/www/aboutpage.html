Gameplay

Eat food that give you energy to run and keep enough distance between you and the lunch lady to avoid being caught.

Objective

Pick the proper food to eat that will keep the nutrition bar balanced to avoid being caught by the lunch lady before time runs out.

Rules

  • Eat food that help you stay ahead of the lunch lady.
  • Red food slows your run.
  • Green food quickens your run.
  • When balance is achieved you will be rewarded with extra points, unlockables, and super avatar power.

Scoring

When you eat, you score!
